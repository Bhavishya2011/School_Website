  • Global Indian School Ajman received "eSafe Child Online Protection Award-2022"

    Global Indian School Ajman received "eSafe Child Online Protection Award-2022" in the International Conference on “Children's Well-being in a Digital World” held on Monday, 6th February 2023 at Anantara Eastern Mangroves Hotel, Abu Dhabi. Out of the 48 qualified nominations in the second round, our school was one of the 10 winners. The selection was based on the following criteria: Formation of Online Safety Team,Awareness Sessions Conducted by the School to the Students, Parents and Teachers, Policy building and implementation for Data Protection, Password Protection, Policy building and implementation of IT Infrastructure Security. The award panel, chaired by Eng. Adel Salem Alkaff Al Hashmi, eSafe Conference Chairman & Vice Chairman of Emirates Safer Internet Society,along with the panel members Dr. Saeed Khalfan Al Dhaheri, Sanjana Bhardwaj and Mr. Jayesh Sebastian.
